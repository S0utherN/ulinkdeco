Company Profile

As a distinguished subsidiary of U LINK, ULINK CAR DECO embodies the essence of luxury and precision. Nestled in the heart of London, we specialize in the bespoke customization and transformation of Mercedes-Benz vehicle seating. Drawing inspiration from U LINK's reputation for offering premium services that emphasize convenience, comfort, and security, CAR DECO takes these principles to the next level by tailoring them to the intricate world of automotive luxury.

Our master craftsmen blend traditional techniques with contemporary aesthetics to redefine the driving experience. We understand that a Mercedes-Benz is not just a car; it's an extension of one's personality and a testament to one's taste. Our mission is to enhance this experience by adding a touch of personalization that truly resonates with the owner.

Double Seat

Step into a realm of shared luxury with the Sophisticated Mercedes-Benz Vclass Double Seats. The double seats exude opulence, offering a six-way power adjustment system to perfectly contour to your body's natural posture, ensuring an exemplary level of comfort. Recline up to 60 degrees for an executive comfort experience, ideal for relaxation or informal discussions. The flexible folding table serves as a convenient space for work or leisure, demonstrating a harmonious blend of functionality and luxury. The seating configuration allows for up to six passengers without sacrificing an iota of luxury, proving the Vclass double seats are indeed a hallmark of Mercedes' innovative luxury.

Elevate your journey with the Mercedes-Benz Vclass Center Control Armrest Box. Designed with a keen eye for detail, the armrest box is not just a storage space but a symbol of luxury and utility.
